Welcome to the snake game.

What is the main goal ?

The goal is to make the best score you can by eating the food which appears randomly in the garden.

How to play ?

You can control the snake by clicking on the directionnal arrows. The snake goes automatically further on the direction you indicated.

The snake is divided into two parts: the head, which is red, and the body, which is dark green.

The food on purple can only be eaten by passing away the head snake onto the food.

When the game stops ?

There are 3 possible ways to stop the game :

You can stop the game by clicking the leave button at the top right corner.

You lose the game when the snake's head or body touches the garden's boundaries.

You lose the game if the snake's head touches its body. In this case, the snake bites its tail.

Be careful: if the snake moves on a direction and goes immediately to the opposite, the snake bites its tail.

Now, you all know about this snake game. Have fun !
